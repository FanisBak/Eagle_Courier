Cross Docking

Through an organized transport network and based on specific schedules, we undertake the delivery of our customers' goods in Piraeus & Thessaloniki and its surroundings as well as throughout the rest of Greece.

We have a wide fleet of vehicles (towed, flatbed, three-axle and small trucks) that carry out daily loadings with the possibility of same-day (cross-docking) deliveries.

The application of the cross-docking system significantly simplifies the supply chain since it reduces storage times with the main goal of Shipping, which is the products to be delivered on the same day of their receipt.
